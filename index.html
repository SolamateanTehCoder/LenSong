Investor Q&A

Why choose "Len Song" as the name?

"Lên sóng" in Vietnamese literally means "to go on air" or "to go live." It’s a nod to the original "Air Up" brand while using a local idiom that resonates with our Gen Z target audience.

Why focus on "Vietnamese Soap Opera" style ads?

These melodrama style ads are a massive trend—highly shareable, humorous, and culturally specific. It makes our brand feel like a "local friend" rather than a foreign invader.

Is 500,000 Dong affordable?

Yes. High-end reusable bottles in Vietnam often sit in the 400k–700k range. As a fashionable lifestyle brand, this price point reflects its status.

Where does the profit gap go (284% to 23.8%)?

The 284% is Gross Profit. The 23.8% Net Profit accounts for marketing spend on live channels, shipping for the online-only model, and local team wages.
